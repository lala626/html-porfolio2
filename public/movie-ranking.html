The Best Movies According to Lala

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Lilo and Stitch

This is a very cute movie, about an alien that landed on earth and found a human family in the end.

The Notebook

This movie explains that love is unconditional, it you are meant to be, God will always make a way. For me this is the best love story movie of all time.

Serendipity

A romantic movie that tells people to believe in faith.
